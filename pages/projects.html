OUR PROJECTS

From Ideas to Impact

Every initiative at Nepashya Foundation is designed to create sustainable, measurable and meaningful change. Rather than isolated events, our projects are part of a long-term vision for stronger communities.

OUR APPROACH

Every Project Follows One Journey

1

Understand

We begin by understanding the actual needs of the community.

2

Educate

Knowledge creates awareness. Awareness creates action.

3

Empower

We help people become capable of solving problems independently.

4

Sustain

Our goal is lasting impact, not one-time activities.

FUTURE VISION

Projects We Aim to Build

01

Community Learning Centre

A local learning space for children, youth and adults where education, digital literacy, values and life skills can be taught regularly.

02

Women Health & Dignity Program

Regular awareness sessions focused on women’s health, menstrual hygiene, preventive care, family support and dignity.

03

Youth Skill & Employment Bridge

Connecting local youth with nearby companies, trainers and practical employment-oriented skills.

04

Environment & Cleanliness Drive

Plantation, cleanliness, waste awareness and responsible environmental behaviour in schools and communities.
