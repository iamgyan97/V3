ABOUT NEPASHYA FOUNDATION

Building Responsible Citizens, Not Just Running Programs

Nepashya Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organisation dedicated to creating positive social change through education, ethical values, skill development, women empowerment, environmental responsibility and community participation.

OUR STORY

Why Nepashya Foundation Was Created

Every meaningful change begins with a question. Why do social problems continue to exist despite development? Why do many talented young people fail to reach their potential? Why is there still a gap between awareness and action? Nepashya Foundation was established to address these questions by creating opportunities where knowledge, ethics and compassion work together. Rather than providing temporary solutions, our aim is to empower individuals so they can become responsible citizens capable of improving their own communities.

WHY THE NAME "NEPASHYA"

A Vision Beyond Today

The word "Nepashya" represents looking beyond the visible present and preparing for a better future. Our foundation believes that sustainable change requires long-term thinking, continuous learning and collective responsibility. Instead of focusing only on today's challenges, we work towards building stronger generations for tomorrow.
